Adidas AG (adidas) designs, manufactures and markets athletic and sports lifestyle products. The company's product portfolio includes footwear, apparel and accessories such as bags, sun glasses, fitness equipment, and balls.

Adidas was founded in 1924 in Germany. Its headquarters are now located in Herzogenaurach, Germany. Adidas specializes in the industry of footwear and textile related to all genders, sizes and sports. Thomas Rabe is the current chairman of the company.

In 2018, it was reported that Adidas had a total revenue of almost 22 billion USD.

Adidas manufactures a range of clothing items, varying from men's and women's t-shirts, jackets, hoodies, pants and leggings. The main focus of Adidas has always been football kits, remaining a major company in the global supply of kits for international football teams and clubs.

Our mission The purpose of creating and expanding the network of sportwear of Adidas - to make our customers comfortable in their footwear and for extra insurance in sport.

Adidas values its image, obligations and fulfillments to employees, partners and states, which in return sets high business standards.
